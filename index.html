About:

The Department offers three programmes BTech in AIML, BTech CSE Specialization in Data Science, BTech in CSE Specialization in AIML and MTech in Data Science.It focuses on equipping students with strong theoretical foundations and practical skills in areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, data analytics, and intelligent systems.The curriculum is designed to meet industry demands and foster innovation, creativity, and critical thinking.With experienced faculty, modern laboratories, and industry collaborations, the department offers a rich learning environment for research and development.Students are encouraged to participate in internships, real-time projects and hackathons to gain hands-on experience.The department also promotes ethical AI practices through societal impact awareness. Graduates are well-prepared for careers in data science, AI research, software development, and analytics across various sectors, including healthcare, finance, education, robotics, and technologies under the Gambit of AI, ML and Data Science, making them valuable contributors to the digital transformation of society. The department conducts workshops and industry hands-on sessions on regular basis to equip students to learn and implement latest technologies in AI and data science.

VISION:To excel in human-centred AI and data-driven innovation through ethical research, societal well-being, and transformative collaborations.
MISSION:Empowering individuals to ethically harness data and AI through accessible and value-driven curriculum.Foster a dynamic research environment that advances innovative and impactful solutions for the betterment of global well-being.
